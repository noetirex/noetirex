Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026 · Version 1.0 · Noetirex

Summary. Speech Buddy is designed for privacy: everything stays on your device. We operate no servers, and the app sends no data to us or to any analytics or advertising service. The app contains no ads and no third-party tracking.

1. Information the App Uses

Account. If you sign in with Google or Apple, the app receives your email address and name (with your consent, via the sign-in provider) and stores them only on your device. If you use demo sign-in, the email and optional first name you type are stored only on your device.

Progress. Lesson progress (stars) is stored only on your device, keyed to the signed-in account.

Nothing else is collected. The app does not access the microphone, camera, contacts, or location, and does not use cookies, analytics, or advertising SDKs.

2. Children's Privacy

Accounts are created by adults; the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. A child's first name may optionally be entered by the parent to personalize greetings; it is stored only on the device and never transmitted to us or anyone else. We comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws by design: no personal information from children is transmitted off the device.

3. Google & Apple Sign-In

When you choose to sign in, authentication happens directly between your device and the provider using the industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol. The app receives only your email address and name, and does not receive your password or access to your mail, contacts, or any other account data. Google's and Apple's own privacy policies govern their handling of your sign-in.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your or your child's personal information with anyone. Because the app keeps all data on your device and operates no servers, we have no access to your data at all.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data lives on your device. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using “Delete Account & Data” inside the app, or by deleting the app from your device. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

6. Security

Sign-in uses HTTPS and OAuth 2.0 with PKCE. The app stores no passwords and retains no sign-in tokens after authentication completes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Because your data is stored only on your device and under your control, you can exercise these rights directly in the app. For any privacy question or request, contact contact@noetirex.store.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be shown in the app and here with a new effective date.
